# **Balance**

Balance is a comprehensive health and wellness application that offers a range of features to help users lead a balanced and healthy life.

**Health Tracking:** The app's first tab is dedicated to health tracking. Users can monitor their daily activities, including calories burned and the number of steps taken. Additionally, they can keep an eye on their heart rate trends for a holistic view of their physical well-being.

**Recipe and Food Search:** The second tab provides users with a recipe and food search feature. This allows them to discover and plan nutritious meals to support their fitness goals and overall health.

**Mental Health Logging:** The third tab focuses on mental health and well-being. Users can log their daily moods using custom emojis, providing a visual representation of their emotional state over time. The app also delivers a daily dose of inspiration through quotable quotes.

**Profile Management:** Balance includes a user profile section that offers both login and sign-up capabilities. It utilizes Firebase for secure authentication and SwiftData for efficient data storage and management.

With its combination of physical health tracking, nutritional support, mental well-being logging, and user profile management, Balance is a versatile and user-friendly app designed to empower individuals to lead a more balanced and healthier lifestyle.
